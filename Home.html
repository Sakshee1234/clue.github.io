ABOUT THE GAME

CLUEDO, also known as CLUE, is the classic mystery game. WHO, with WHAT weapon and WHERE!? Download the official app and be the first to crack the case! Join Miss Scarlett, Colonel Mustard, Mrs Peacock, Reverend Green, Dr Orchid and Professor Plum on a night of murder and mystery. A selection of different boards will have you sleuthing your way through historic and fantastical locations, with the help of an innovative logic-assisted virtual Clue Sheet to take notes and eliminate red herrings. Whodunit? Only you can find out!
